A Guide to the Universities of the World

This WebSite features guide to International Students attempting to pursue degrees and wishing to experience campus abroad.

Featured Universities:

Vienna University campus

The University of Vienna is a public university located in Vienna, Austria. It was founded by Duke Rudolph IV in 1365 and is the oldest university in the German-speaking world.

University of Cambridge

Cambridge is an university with a history running nearly half a millenium. It is one of the most prestigious universities to set foot into.

MIT-Massechesseuts Institute of Technology

One of the Ivy League colleges in the US, most sought after for technical degrees and diplomas.

University of Modena

The University of Modena and Reggio Emilia located in Modena and Reggio Emilia, Emilia-Romagna, Italy, is one of the oldest and most prestigious universities in Italy, founded in 1175, with a population of 20,000 students.
